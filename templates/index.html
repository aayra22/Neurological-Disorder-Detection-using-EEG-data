Neurological

Disorder Detection

Detecting neurological disorders through EEG data analysis is a revolutionary approach in modern healthcare. By leveraging advanced signal processing , EEG readings can reveal hidden patterns linked to epilepsy, Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, and other brain disorders. Machine learning models analyze brainwave abnormalities, enabling early diagnosis and precise treatment planning. This non-invasive technique enhances accuracy, reducing the need for costly and complex procedures. Real-time monitoring of brain activity empowers neurologists with valuable insights for personalized care.
